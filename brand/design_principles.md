# Brand & Design Principles\n\n*Based on the GWCproduct-marketing-context.md*\n\n## Colors (Proposed based on "Earthy" and "Plant-Forward" vibe)\n- **Primary**: Deep Forest Green (Earthy, grounding)\n- **Secondary**: Sage or Moss Green (Calming, refreshing)\n- **Accent**: Warm Gold or Terracotta (Inviting, warm cafe energy)\n- **Background**: Soft Cream or Off-White (Clean, readable, natural)\n- **Text**: Dark Charcoal (Softer than pure black, accessible)\n\n## Typography (The Elegant Coven Pairing)\n- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (An elegant, slightly dramatic serif evoking old herbal medicine books).\n- **Body**: Outfit (A highly modern, geometric sans-serif for high readability on mobile devices).\n\n## Brand Vibe / Voice\n- **Tone**: Nurturing, earthy, knowledgeable, and inviting.\n- **Personality**: Earthy, kind, "Cool Witch," Nurturing.\n- **Key Descriptors**: Peaceful, refreshing, intentional, quaint, calming.\n- **Atmosphere to Convey**: A quiet, cozy community destination with a metaphysical touch (sage/incense aromas, retail plants).\n
