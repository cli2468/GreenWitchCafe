# Findings\n\n## 2026-03-07: Brand Context Analysis\n- **Identified Brand Identity**: Green Witch Cafe is a plant-forward, vegan/vegetarian community cafe in Highland, Indiana.\n- **Core Vibe**: "Cool Witch", earthy, nurturing, peaceful.\n- **Differentiators**: Intentional vegan menu (not just substitutes), metaphysical retail items (plants, gifts), owner-operated high hospitality.\n- **Target Audience**: Health-conscious locals, remote workers looking for a quiet space.\n- **Key Design Impact**: The website needs to feel "refreshing" and "calming" immediately. We will use earthy colors (greens, warm accents) and metaphysical touches in the design. Copy will avoid fast-food terminology and focus on nourishment and community.\n
